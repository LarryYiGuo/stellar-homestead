# Gameplay Guide · 玩法说明(v4 · 物流纪元)

---

## English

### Core loop

```
Pick routes & haul (ore out, supplies & settlers in) ──→ colonies get what they need
      ↑                                                        │
      │                                                        ▼
farther systems (risk = battles) ←── engine/car upgrades ←── YOU plan districts & buildings
      │                                                        │
      ▼                                                        ▼
loot, unique weapons, officers                colonies self-grow: population ↑ output ↑
      │                                       research ↑ influence ↑ … and new DEMAND
      └────────────── the supply gap is the next job for your train ←──────────┘
```

Development is **never a timer**. A planet's level = districts built + buildings built
+ population milestones + (for mining worlds) cumulative exports + (for habitable worlds)
settlers delivered. Everything routes back to decisions you make.

### Colonies (Stellaris-style economy sheet)

- **Population** grows logistically on its own (habitability × food factor), toward a housing
  cap set by *habitation districts*. It is the master multiplier: consumption tiers, dev
  points, workforce buffs all key off it.
- **Consumption**: pops eat **consumer goods (compounds)**; worlds below 70% habitability
  also burn **water ice** for life support; industry/research/arsenal districts (beyond the
  first) burn **helium-3**. All drawn from the planet's **local store** — when it runs dry,
  growth stalls and output halves. The red numbers in the planet panel are your freight
  orders.
- **You build everything**: pick district types (costs escalate per district; environment
  rules apply — harsh worlds only unmanned industry/research at ×3 efficiency, first arsenal
  district must sit on a moon, galaxy-wide non-habitation ≤ habitation×2+4) and choose each
  building. One construction slot per planet; docking your train doubles construction speed;
  resources can rush-finish ("注资"), influence can rush remotely.
- **Mining worlds** produce into their local store (capped — trade districts extend it).
  Output only counts when it *leaves*: exports push the planet's development level.

### The train & logistics (three tiers of automation)

- The cargo hold is physical: collect ore into the hold (45 s cooldown), then either
  **unload supplies** at a colony (only consumables) or **bank** the hold into your treasury
  at any starport / your home port. Lose a cargo car in battle and part of the hold spills.
- **Trade lines** (same system, both ends with starports) automate a route you've proven;
  **stargates** (late game) automate whole regions with collection circles and relays.
  Train → lines → gates: the same demand, progressively automated.
- Engine LV (max 10) gates deep-space systems; car slots come from total colony development.
  All upgrade timers are minutes, not days.

### Battles

Turn-based deck battler, unchanged in structure: enemy intents are public, 4-card hands,
command points, escape always available. Hazard regions scale enemy strength and loot —
risk is a route decision now that loot is a serious income stream. Boss fleets guard the
deepest systems; unique weapons drop from warlords and bosses.

### Starter tips

- Minute one: open a district on Canglan (you choose what), send the train to Jinyan with
  1,000 migrants, and start the metal flowing home.
- Watch the supply lines: consumer goods run out in about two hours at start — Shamu
  (compounds) is your first trade-line candidate.
- Exports level mining worlds; settlers level habitable worlds. Haul accordingly.

## 中文

### 核心循环

```
选航线拉货(矿石运出 · 补给/移民运入)──→ 殖民地得到它需要的东西
      ↑                                            │
      │                                            ▼
更远星系(风险=战斗)←── 引擎/车厢升级 ←── 你亲手规划区划与建筑
      │                                            │
      ▼                                            ▼
战利品 · 具名武器 · 船官            殖民地自生长:人口↑ 产出↑ 科研↑ 影响力↑
      │                                     ……以及新的需求缺口
      └────────── 缺口就是列车的下一单生意 ←──────────┘
```

开发度**不再是计时器**。星球等级 = 建成区划 + 建成建筑 + 人口里程碑
+(资源星)累计出口 +(居住星)落户移民 —— 全部回到你的决策。

### 殖民(群星式收支面板)

- **人口**自行增长(宜居度 × 物资系数,逻辑斯谛曲线),上限由**民生区**决定。
  人口是总乘数:消耗档位、开发点、劳动力红利都由它驱动。
- **消耗**:人口吃**消费品(大气化合物)**;宜居度低于 70% 的星球还要烧**水冰**维持生命支持;
  工/研/军区划(首座自供)烧**氦-3**。全部从**本地仓**扣 —— 断供则增长停滞、产出减半。
  行星面板上的红字,就是你的运单。
- **一切由你建**:选区划类型(成本逐座递增;环境规则:严酷星只许无人工业/科研但效率 ×3、
  首个军工区必须建在卫星、全银河非居住区划 ≤ 民生×2+4),建筑逐个点建。
  每星一个施工位;列车驻留建设 ×2;资源可「注资完工」,影响力可远程动员。
- **资源星**产出进本地仓(有上限,商贸区扩容)。产出**运出去才算数**:出口量直接推动开发等级。

### 列车与物流(自动化三级跳)

- 货舱是实体的:收取矿石进货舱(冷却 45 秒),再到殖民地「卸货补给」(只卸消耗品)
  或在星港/母港「入库金库」。战斗中货运车厢被打瘫,舱内货物会洒落。
- **贸易线**(同星系、两端有星港)把你跑熟的航线自动化;**星门**(后期)用收集圈+接力
  把整个星区自动化。列车 → 贸易线 → 星门:同一种需求,逐级自动化。
- 引擎等级(最高 10)门控深空星系;车厢槽位来自全银河殖民开发总和。
  所有工期都是分钟级,没有天级计时。

### 遭遇战

回合制卡组战斗,结构不变:敌方意图明牌、4 张手牌、指挥点、全速脱离常驻。
星图区域决定敌人强度与战利品 —— 战利品现在是正经收入,风险就是航线决策的一部分。
Boss 舰队把守最深的星系;督军与 Boss 掉落具名武器。

### 上手建议

- 第一分钟:在沧澜开一座区划(类型你选),带 1000 移民去烬岩建前哨,让金属流动起来。
- 盯住补给线:开局的消费品大约够两小时 —— 纱幕(化合物)是你第一条贸易线的候选。
- 出口养资源星,移民养居住星 —— 拉什么货,决定谁升级。
