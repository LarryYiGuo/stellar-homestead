### Colonies

- Each planet develops through five stages (Outpost → Settlement → Colony → Planetary City → Ecological Civilization) purely with **real time** — offline included. Habitability sets the speed.
- **Habitable** planets grow population; **resource** planets accumulate one of five resources (rare metal, atmospheric compounds, helium-3, water ice, deuterium). Output sits in local storage until the train collects it.
- Habitable and resource levels buff each other galaxy-wide; the Civilization Index rewards balanced development.

### The galaxy & the train

- 30 star systems: the home system Kenxi is handcrafted (with the main story); the rest are procedurally generated, each with a specialty resource and a hazard rating. Deep-space systems are gated purely by **engine level (up to LV10)**.
- The train is your only link between systems. Cars: locomotive (speed / range), cargo (haul size), weapon platforms (battle), habitat (command points, cooldowns, defense), engineering (refining bonus).
- Car slots unlock as your **total colony development** grows — every 5 levels grants one more car (max 8).
- Three weapons from the original *Interstellar Train* design: Automatic Gun (double burst), Laser Cannon (crits), Missile Launcher (splash) — unlocked through specific colony milestones, upgradable to LV5.
- Building outposts in remote systems requires the train to be docked there; collecting local output requires it too (limited by cargo capacity, with a cooldown).

### Battles (turn-based auto-battler with deck-building)

- Arriving in hazardous systems can trigger an encounter. Each round has two phases:
  1. **Deploy** — draw 4 cards from your tactic deck and play them with command points (3, +1 with a habitat car). Enemy intents (which car they'll hit, for how much) are fully visible. "Full-speed escape" is always available as a fixed order.
  2. **Resolve** — automatic exchange of fire, animated log.
- Enemies aim at specific cars: siege ships hunt **turrets**, drillers go for the **locomotive**, raiders pick randomly, swarms win by numbers. From hazard 3 up, elites carry affixes (Armored / Swift / Volatile / Regenerating / Sniper / Flagship).
- A car reduced to 0 HP is **disabled** — turrets stop firing, a destroyed cargo car spills loot, a destroyed locomotive means defeat and half speed afterwards. Damage persists after battle until repaired with resources.
- **Deck-building**: an 18-card pool in three rarities. Win a battle and pick one of three new cards (or skip). Some cards need specific train cars; legendary cards are once per battle.
- Three boss fleets guard the deepest systems (guaranteed on first visit, no auto-resolve): the Plunder King (summons reinforcements), the First-Platform Watcher (telegraphed all-car volley), and the Wayfarer "Next Train" (alternating phase shield).
- Don't feel like fighting a normal raid? **Delegate** it — one roll based on firepower + defense.

### Starter tips

- Early on, ship metal home from the nearest red-dwarf system to fund your first extra car.
- Watch enemy intents: shield before a heavy volley, seal the car being focused, EMP the scariest elite.
- Keep habitable and resource levels balanced — the Civilization Index minimum term punishes lopsided empires.

```
      ↑                                                      │
      │                                                      ▼
建立前哨(远征星系需列车驻留)              加挂货舱 / 武器 / 生活 / 工程舱
      ↑                                                      │
      │                                                      ▼
列车抵达新星系 ←── 银河总图选择航线 ←── 引擎升级解锁更远星系
      │
      ▼
靠站收取资源 → 金库 → 武器 / 引擎 / 修复
      │
      ▼
遭遇战(回合制卡组)→ 战利品 & 新指令卡
```

### 殖民

- 每颗星球沿五个阶段发展(前哨站 → 聚居点 → 殖民地 → 行星都市 → 生态文明),只随**真实时间**推进——离线也不停。宜居度决定速度。
- **居住型**星球增长人口;**资源型**星球累积五种资源之一(稀有金属、大气化合物、氦-3、水冰、氘),产出滞留本地仓,需列车靠站收取。
- 居住与资源等级在全银河范围互相加成;文明指数奖励均衡发展。

### 银河与列车

- 30 个星系:母星系垦曦为手作内容(含主线剧情),其余程序生成,各有特产资源与危险度。深空星系纯以**引擎等级(最高 LV10)**门控。
- 列车是星系间唯一的纽带。车厢:动力车头(航速/航程)、货运(装载量)、武器平台(战斗)、生活舱(指挥点/冷却/防御)、工程舱(精炼加成)。
- 车厢槽位随**全银河殖民等级和**解锁——每 5 级 +1 节(上限 8 节)。
- 三种武器沿用《Interstellar Train》原设:机关炮(连射)、激光炮(暴击)、导弹井(溅射)——由特定殖民里程碑解锁,可升至 LV5。
- 远征星系建前哨需列车驻留;收取当地产出同样需要(受货舱容量限制,有冷却)。

### 遭遇战(回合制自走棋 + 卡组构筑)

- 抵达高危星系概率触发。每回合两段:
  1. **部署**——从指令卡组抽 4 张手牌,用指挥点(3,有生活舱 +1)打出;敌舰意图(打哪节车厢、多少伤害)完全明牌。"全速脱离"是常驻固定指令。
  2. **结算**——自动交火,逐条战报动画。
- 敌方打部位:破城炮舰专轰**炮塔**、钻头舱直扑**车头**、掠袭艇随机、蜂群以量取胜。危险度 ≥3 出现精英词缀(装甲/迅捷/自爆/自愈/狙击/旗舰)。
- 车厢 HP 归零即**瘫痪**——炮塔哑火、货舱破裂掉战利品、车头被毁直接战败且战后半速。击伤战后保留,需花资源修复。
- **卡组构筑**:18 张卡池、三档稀有度。胜利后三选一入组(可跳过);部分卡需要对应车厢;传奇卡每场限一次。
- 三场 Boss 战守在最深处的星系(首访必战,不可委托):劫掠王(召唤增援)、第一站台守望者(明牌充能齐射)、环卫者「下一班车」(相位护盾轮替)。
- 普通遭遇不想打可以**委托结算**——按火力+防御一锤定音。

### 上手建议

- 开局先去最近的红矮星系拉金属回家,凑出第一节新车厢。
- 盯着敌方意图打:重击前上护盾、被集火的车厢紧急封舱、最吓人的精英吃电磁脉冲。
- 居住与资源等级保持均衡——文明指数的协同项惩罚偏科。
